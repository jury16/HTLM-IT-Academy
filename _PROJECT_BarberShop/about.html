About us

Here you can always not only have a haircut, shave, but also just relax, meet many interesting people, make new useful acquaintances, discuss serious topics over a cup of black coffee or a glass of excellent whiskey, in a word, spend time in a friendly company.

The masters of our barbershop are professionals of a serious level with impressive experience and the corresponding certificates and diplomas, were trained in the best shaving schools in Canada and Europe.

The primary task is to create a well-groomed and expensive appearance with the hands of first-class barbershop masters, so that the result obtained will please yourself and others. If you want the image to emphasize your status, then you are our client.

We value each client, take into account his tastes and wishes. We are chosen by men of different social status, but carefully following fashion trends. Our main credo is the quality of the services provided.

Our team is a team of talented, self-confident, constantly developing professionals. But the main quality that distinguishes each of our craftsmen is a sincere passion for their work. We are all in love with our work, so we are ready to endlessly improve our skills. Each of us dreams of reaching the most dizzying heights in the profession, so that the images we have created can be safely attributed to works of high art. We are always interested at work, we are proud to work in such a cool place as our barbershop. We share with every visitor a good mood and positive energy, and taking into account the fact that they are very pleased with their own updated images, they come to us again and again.
